Inkwell is a native reader and writer for the Standard.site publishing ecosystem on AT Protocol.

Read site.standard.publication and site.standard.document records directly from the author's PDS. Renders Leaflet block content and Markpub/Offprint/pckt markdown formats. Resolves display themes from Leaflet's rich palette through basicTheme to Material 3 defaults.

Discover publications via the cross-platform public index, then fetches authoritative records from the author. Cross-repo comment discovery via Constellation (microcosm.blue). Paginated feed with infinite scroll.

Write and publish Standard.site documents with portable metadata and selectable content formats.

Authentication is OAuth-based with your AT Protocol handle — no app password required. Silent session restoration on relaunch.

All code is AGPL-3.0.
